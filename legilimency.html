Legilimency: The Art of Mind Reading

In the magical world, Legilimency is the mystical skill of navigating another person's thoughts and memories. It goes beyond simply reading emotions — a skilled Legilimens can delve into a person’s mind, uncover hidden truths, and even influence perception.

Practicing Legilimency requires intense focus, magical training, and an understanding of how to interpret thoughts as if reading a complex book. In contrast, Occlumency is the magical defense against such intrusions.

Whether you're studying the concept in theory or seeing how it might metaphorically apply to technology, communication, or human behavior — it's a fascinating area that bridges empathy, psychology, and magical intuition.
