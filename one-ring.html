Exploring the Ethical and Privacy Dilemmas of Middle-earth’s Most Dangerous Artifact

Introduction to the One Ring

The One Ring is perhaps the most infamous magical artifact in all of Middle-earth. Forged by Sauron in secret, the Ring grants invisibility to its wearer but also connects them to the Dark Lord’s ever-watchful Eye. While it offers power, it simultaneously compromises the privacy and autonomy of its bearer. This dual nature reflects modern challenges where tools meant to empower can also expose us to surveillance and control.
